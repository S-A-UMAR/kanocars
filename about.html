Our Story

Redefining Luxury Procurement in Nigeria

Founded with a vision to eliminate the anxieties of high-end car buying, AUNA has grown to become Kano's most trusted luxury dealership. We specialize in importing certified foreign used vehicles that are fully customs-cleared and thoroughly inspected for Kano's elite.

We understand that buying a luxury vehicle is an investment. That is why our core philosophy is built on absolute transparency — meaning no rollback mileages, full disclosure of vehicle history, and pre-sales inspection reports.
